Spider Librarian Iteration 1

Controls:
  • On the ground: a/d move you left and right and space jumps, Click the mouse to try and create a web line and press e to try to attach to a web line near your cursor.
  • On a Web: A/D to move left or right along the web, spacebar to dettach.
  • Press R to interact when your within an interaction hitbox (Semi Transparent)

Score points by bringning books of the correct section to the turn in (Green interactalble zone).
